Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is an amazing dish that is pasta as well as meat and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Olive Oil
  • Bacon slices
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Oregano
  • Ground beef
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Crushes tomatoes
  • Tomato paste
  • White sugar
  • Salt and ground black pepper
  • Fresh basil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti
  2. Heal the olive oil and put all ingredients in
  3. Pour balsamic vinegar over the ground beef
  4. Ladle the sauce over the cooked spaghetti
